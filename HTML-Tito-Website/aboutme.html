Who am I?

The dog-savvy folks out there will know from my picture that I am a Cavalier King Charles Spaniel.

These dogs were originally bred as lap dogs to keep people warm in the days before everybody had central heating.

I consider myself very good at my job! I absolutely love sitting on or near my owner.
